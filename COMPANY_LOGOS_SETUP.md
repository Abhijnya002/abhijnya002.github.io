# Company Logos Setup for Experience Section

## Logo Files Needed

Place the following logo files in `/public/images/logos/`:

1. **syracuse-university.png** - Syracuse University logo
2. **mercedes-benz.png** - Mercedes-Benz logo  
3. **default-company.png** - Default fallback logo

## Where to Get Logos

1. **Official Company Websites**
   - Visit company websites and look for press/media resources
   - Often found in footer or "About" sections

2. **Logo Search Engines** (for reference/guidance)
   - logos-world.net
   - seeklogo.com
   - brandfetch.com

3. **Create Simple Text Logos**
   - If official logos aren't available, create simple text-based logos
   - Use company colors and fonts
   - Keep it clean and professional

## Logo Specifications

- **Format**: PNG with transparent background (preferred) or JPG with white background
- **Size**: 200x200px to 400x400px (square aspect ratio works best)
- **Background**: Transparent (PNG) or white (JPG)
- **Style**: Official company logos work best
