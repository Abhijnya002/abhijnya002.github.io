# Company Logos

Place company logo images here for the Experience section.

## Required Logos:
- `syracuse-university.png` - Syracuse University logo
- `mercedes-benz.png` - Mercedes-Benz logo
- `default-company.png` - Default company logo (fallback)

## Image Recommendations:
- Format: PNG with transparent background (preferred) or JPG
- Size: 200x200px to 400x400px
- Background: Transparent or white
- Style: Official company logos work best

You can download logos from:
- Company official websites
- Logo search engines (like logos-world.net, seeklogo.com)
- Or create simple text-based logos if official logos aren't available
